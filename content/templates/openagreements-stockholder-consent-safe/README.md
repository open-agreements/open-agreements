# Stockholder Consent for SAFE Financing

Action by written consent of the stockholders of a Delaware corporation
approving the issuance of one or more Simple Agreements for Future Equity
(SAFEs).

## Notes

- This consent is scoped to **Delaware corporations** and references Section
  228 of the Delaware General Corporation Law.
- Resolutions become effective only after the required board approvals, with a
  **60-day maximum window** from the earliest delivery of the written consent.
- The consent authorizes **one or more SAFEs** in a round. Individual investor
  names and economic terms remain in each SAFE itself, not this stockholder
  consent.
- The consent does **not** cover concurrent charter amendments, conversions of
  outstanding convertible notes, or side letters granting pro rata or
  information rights. Consult counsel for those matters.
