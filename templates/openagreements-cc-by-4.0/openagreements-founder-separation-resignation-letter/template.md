### Resignation

### Irrevocable and Complete

This resignation is irrevocable and covers every officer and director position I hold with the Company, whether or not listed above. It is not conditioned on any action by the Company and takes effect on the Effective Date without acceptance by the Board.

This letter does not create, and shall not be read to create, any new non-competition, non-solicitation, or other post-separation restrictive covenant; any such covenant, if any, exists only under a separate written agreement.
