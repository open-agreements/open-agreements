LIMITED PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT
OF
SFUMATO STEWARDSHIP LP

This Limited Partnership Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of March 9, 2026,
by and among Sfumato Stewardship GP LLC, a Delaware limited liability company, as the
General Partner, and the persons listed on Exhibit A hereto as Limited Partners.

ARTICLE V — MANAGEMENT

5.3 Limitation of Liability. The General Partner shall not be liable to any Limited
Partner for any act taken in good faith, except for fraud or willful misconduct.

ARTICLE IX — TRANSFERS

9.1 No Limited Partner may transfer its Interest without the prior written consent
of the General Partner.

ARTICLE XIV — MISCELLANEOUS

14.5 Confidentiality. Each Limited Partner shall keep confidential all information
regarding the Partnership and its investments.

14.10 Governing Law. This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of
Delaware.
