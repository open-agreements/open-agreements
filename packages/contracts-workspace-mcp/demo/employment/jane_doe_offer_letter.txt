Dear Jane,

On behalf of TechStartup Inc. (the "Company"), I am pleased to extend this offer
of employment to you for the position of Vice President of Engineering, reporting
to Sarah Chen, Chief Executive Officer. We believe your experience and skills will
be a valuable addition to our team.

1. POSITION AND START DATE

Your anticipated start date is November 1, 2025. You will work out of our
San Francisco headquarters. This is a full-time, exempt position.

2. COMPENSATION

2.1 Base Salary. Your annual base salary will be Two Hundred Forty Thousand Dollars
($240,000.00), payable in accordance with the Company's standard payroll schedule,
currently semi-monthly, less applicable withholdings and deductions.

2.2 Annual Bonus. You will be eligible for an annual performance bonus of up to
thirty percent (30%) of your base salary, based on the achievement of individual
and Company performance goals to be established by the CEO. Your bonus for the
first year will be prorated based on your start date.

2.3 Equity. Subject to approval by the Company's Board of Directors, you will be
granted a stock option to purchase 150,000 shares of the Company's common stock at
the fair market value on the date of grant. The option will vest over four (4) years,
with twenty-five percent (25%) vesting on the first anniversary of your start date
and the remainder vesting in equal monthly installments over the following thirty-six
(36) months, subject to your continued employment. The option will be governed by
the Company's 2024 Equity Incentive Plan and the applicable stock option agreement.

3. BENEFITS

3.1 You will be eligible to participate in the Company's employee benefit plans,
including medical, dental, and vision insurance, 401(k) plan, and other benefits
as may be offered to similarly situated employees from time to time. Details of
these benefits will be provided during your onboarding.

3.2 You will accrue Paid Time Off ("PTO") at the rate of twenty (20) days per year,
in addition to Company-observed holidays.

4. AT-WILL EMPLOYMENT

Your employment with the Company is "at-will," meaning that either you or the
Company may terminate the employment relationship at any time, with or without
cause, and with or without notice. Nothing in this letter or any other document
shall be construed as a guarantee of continued employment for any specific period.

5. SEVERANCE

5.1 If the Company terminates your employment without Cause (as defined below)
during the first twenty-four (24) months of your employment, you will be entitled
to receive: (a) continued payment of your base salary for six (6) months following
the termination date; and (b) payment of COBRA premiums for up to six (6) months,
subject to your execution and non-revocation of a separation agreement and general
release of claims in a form provided by the Company.

5.2 "Cause" means: (a) material breach of any Company policy or this agreement;
(b) conviction of a felony or crime of moral turpitude; (c) willful misconduct or
gross negligence in the performance of your duties; or (d) material failure to
perform your duties after written notice and a reasonable opportunity to cure.

6. CONFIDENTIALITY AND IP ASSIGNMENT

As a condition of your employment, you will be required to sign the Company's
standard Confidential Information and Invention Assignment Agreement ("CIIAA")
on or before your start date. A copy of the CIIAA is enclosed with this letter.

7. NON-SOLICITATION

During your employment and for a period of twelve (12) months following the
termination of your employment for any reason, you agree that you will not,
directly or indirectly, solicit, recruit, or encourage any employee of the Company
to leave the Company's employment, or solicit or divert any customer or prospective
customer of the Company.

8. REPRESENTATIONS

By accepting this offer, you represent that: (a) you are not subject to any
non-competition or other restrictive covenant that would prevent you from performing
your duties; (b) you will not bring or use any confidential information or trade
secrets of any former employer; and (c) you are legally authorized to work in the
United States.

9. BACKGROUND CHECK

This offer is contingent upon satisfactory completion of a background check and
reference verification. The Company reserves the right to withdraw this offer if
the results of the background check are unsatisfactory.

10. GOVERNING LAW

This offer letter shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws
of the State of California, without regard to its conflict of laws principles.

11. ACCEPTANCE

This offer will remain open until October 15, 2025. To accept, please sign and
return a copy of this letter to hr@techstartup.example.com.

We are very excited about the possibility of you joining TechStartup Inc. and look
forward to working with you.
