# Palelei v. Star-Kist Samoa, Inc.

*Palelei v. Star-Kist Samoa, Inc., 5 A.S.R.2d 162 (Trial Div. 1987).*

Supporting case-law excerpt. Quoted across 1 document(s) in the OpenAgreements legal practice library.

## Quoted passages

### palelei-v-star-kist-samoa-inc-if-the-parties-to-an-employment-bb2e9221 {#palelei-v-star-kist-samoa-inc-if-the-parties-to-an-employment-bb2e9221}

> If the parties to an employment contract have neither fixed a definite term of employment nor created any contractual obstacle to the right of discretionary discharge, then the contract is for employment at will and the employer may discharge the employee without incurring liability.
