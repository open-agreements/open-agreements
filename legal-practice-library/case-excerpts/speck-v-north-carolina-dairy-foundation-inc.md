# Speck v. North Carolina Dairy Foundation, Inc.

### speck-v-north-carolina-dairy-foundation-inc-if-the-employee-fails-to-reach-1cfffb68 {#speck-v-north-carolina-dairy-foundation-inc-if-the-employee-fails-to-reach-1cfffb68}

> If the employee fails to reach his goal the loss falls upon the employer, but if he succeeds in accomplishing the prescribed result then the invention belongs to the employer even though the terms of employment contain no express provision dealing with the ownership of whatever inventions may be developed.

### speck-v-north-carolina-dairy-foundation-inc-the-fruit-of-the-labor-of-e9c742c2 {#speck-v-north-carolina-dairy-foundation-inc-the-fruit-of-the-labor-of-e9c742c2}

> The fruit of the labor of one who is hired to invent, accomplish a prescribed result, or aid in the development of products belongs to the employer absent a written contract to assign.
