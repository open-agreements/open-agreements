# U.S. corporate-governance survey

# Schema

Each row compares one jurisdiction across the survey columns:

- `enforceability` — Are non-competes enforceable?
- `bottomLine` — Summary
- `keyLaw` — Main law or case
- `exceptions` — Main exceptions
- `banEffectiveDate` — When the ban took effect
- `courtNarrowing` — Can a court narrow it?
- `appliesToContractors` — Applies to contractors?
- `extendedForBreach` — Restriction extended during a breach?
- `maxLength` — Maximum length set by law
- `employerPayToEnforce` — Must the employer pay to enforce?
- `noticeRequirement` — Advance notice required?
- `incomeThreshold` — Pay or earnings threshold
- `saleOfBusiness` — Sale-of-business covenants
- `customerNonSolicit` — Customer non-solicits
- `employeeNonSolicit` — Employee non-solicits
- `futureEffective` — Future effective law
