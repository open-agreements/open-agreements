# Emerick v. Cardiac Study Center, Inc.

*Emerick v. Cardiac Study Ctr., Inc., 189 Wn. App. 711 (2015).*

Cited authority (case law). Quoted across 2 practice note(s) in the OpenAgreements legal practice library.

## Quoted propositions

### Quote 1 {#q1}

> If the trial court determines that certain terms of the covenant are unreasonable—such as the geographic and temporal scope of the restraint—the entire covenant does not fail.

- **Supports:** `court-narrowing`, `severability-and-reformation`

### Quote 2 {#q2}

> Under Washington law, noncompete covenants are enforceable if they are reasonable and lawful.
