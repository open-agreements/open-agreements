# Hilb, Rogal & Hamilton Co. of Arizona v. McKinney

*Hilb, Rogal & Hamilton Co. of Arizona v. McKinney, 190 Ariz. 213, 946 P.2d 464 (Ct. App. 1997).*

Cited authority (case law). Quoted across 2 practice note(s) in the OpenAgreements legal practice library.

## Quoted propositions

### Quote 1 {#q1}

> A restrictive covenant - whether a covenant not to compete or an anti-piracy agreement - is enforceable as long as it is no broader than necessary to protect the employer's legitimate business interest.
