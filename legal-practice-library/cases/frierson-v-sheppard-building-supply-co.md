# Frierson v. Sheppard Building Supply Co.

*Frierson v. Sheppard Bldg. Supply Co., 247 Miss. 157, 154 So. 2d 151 (Miss. 1963).*

Cited authority (case law). Quoted across 2 practice note(s) in the OpenAgreements legal practice library.

## Quoted propositions

### Quote 1 {#q1}

> Having made the contract limiting the period to two years from the date of termination of employment, the court may not extend the time beyond that date.
