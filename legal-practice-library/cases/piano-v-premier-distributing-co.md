# Piano v. Premier Distributing Co.

*Piano v. Premier Distrib. Co., 2005-NMCA-018, 107 P.3d 11.*

Cited authority (case law). Quoted across 2 practice note(s) in the OpenAgreements legal practice library.

## Quoted propositions

### Quote 1 {#q1}

> A legally enforceable contract requires evidence supporting the existence of “an offer, an acceptance, consideration, and mutual assent.”
