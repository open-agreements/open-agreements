# Van Dyck Printing Co. v. DiNicola

*Van Dyck Printing Co. v. DiNicola, 43 Conn. Supp. 191 (1993).*

Cited authority (case law). Quoted across 2 practice note(s) in the OpenAgreements legal practice library.

## Quoted propositions

### Quote 1 {#q1}

> A restriction covering a large area might be reasonable if in effect for a brief time, while a restriction covering a small area might be reasonable for a longer time.
