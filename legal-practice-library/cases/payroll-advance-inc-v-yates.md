# Payroll Advance, Inc. v. Yates

*Payroll Advance, Inc. v. Yates, 270 S.W.3d 428 (Mo. App. S.D. 2008).*

Cited authority (case law). Quoted across 2 practice note(s) in the OpenAgreements legal practice library.

## Quoted propositions

### Quote 1 {#q1}

> Generally, because covenants not to compete are considered to be restraints on trade, they are presumptively void and are enforceable only to the extent that they are demonstratively reasonable.
