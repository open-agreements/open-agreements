# AECI Australia Pty Ltd v Convey

*AECI Australia Pty Ltd v Convey [2020] QSC 207 (Bradley J), quoting Just Group Ltd v Peck (2016) 344 ALR 162.*

Cited authority (case law). Quoted across 1 practice note(s) in the OpenAgreements legal practice library.

## Quoted propositions

### Quote 1 {#q1}

> a purchaser of a business is entitled to protect itself from competition by the vendor; but an employer is not entitled to protect itself from competition per se by an employee.

- **Supports:** `enforceability`
