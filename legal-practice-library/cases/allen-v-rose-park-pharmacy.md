# Allen v. Rose Park Pharmacy

*Allen v. Rose Park Pharmacy, 237 P.2d 823 (Utah 1951).*

Cited authority (case law). Quoted across 2 practice note(s) in the OpenAgreements legal practice library.

## Quoted propositions

### Quote 1 {#q1}

> a contract does not lack mutuality merely because its terms are harsh or its obligations unequal, or because every obligation of one party is not met by an equivalent counter obligation of the other party.
