# Velega v. Legislature of American Samoa

*Velega v. Legislature of American Samoa, 4 A.S.R.3d 145 (Trial Div. 2000).*

Cited authority (case law). Quoted across 1 practice note(s) in the OpenAgreements legal practice library.

## Quoted propositions

### Quote 1 {#q1}

> In American Samoa, employment is presumed to be at will, and the employee can be fired without just or good cause, unless there exists an express or implied contractual provision restricting the employer's rights to terminate the employee.
