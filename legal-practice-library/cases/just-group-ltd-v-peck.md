# Just Group Ltd v Peck

*Just Group Ltd v Peck [2016] VSCA 334 (the Court).*

Cited authority (case law). Quoted across 1 practice note(s) in the OpenAgreements legal practice library.

## Quoted propositions

### Quote 1 {#q1}

> A term in a contract, which is a restraint of trade ('a restraint clause'), is presumed to be void as contrary to public policy.

- **Supports:** `enforceability`, `federal-reform`
- **Cited by:** [Non-Compete Enforceability in Victoria](../non-compete/australia-victoria.md)

### Quote 2 {#q2}

> However, it is not permissible for a court to read down an unduly wide clause in order to preserve its validity.

- **Supports:** `cascading-clauses`, `court-narrowing`, `tolling`
